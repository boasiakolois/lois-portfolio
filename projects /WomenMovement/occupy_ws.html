Occupy Wall Street Movement

The Occupy Wall Street (OWS) movement began on September 17, 2011, in New York City's Zuccotti Park, in the Financial District of Manhattan. It was a grassroots protest aimed at addressing economic inequality, corporate greed, and the disproportionate influence of corporations on government. The movement was inspired by the broader Occupy Movement and influenced by events like the Arab Spring and anti-austerity protests in Europe.
